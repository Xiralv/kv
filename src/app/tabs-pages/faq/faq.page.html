Guests & RSVP

Can I bring a plus one?

Your invitation is made out to the specific guests listed on your RSVP. If a plus one is included, they'll appear in the RSVP flow when you enter your name. If they're not listed, we kindly ask that only the named guests attend — we have a set number of seats.

On the day

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony begins at 3:00 PM sharp — please don't be late! We recommend arriving at least 15–20 minutes early to find your seat.

 Can I take photos during the ceremony?

Yes, photos are very much welcome! Just please be mindful not to block the aisle or step in front of our documentation team — we have official photographers and videographers on-site capturing every moment.

Getting there

Is parking available?

Our Mother of Perpetual Help Parish has ample space for parking. You can also book Grab or a taxi to the church so you have less to worry about. Waterfront Insular Hotel has its own parking for the reception.

Gifts & contact

Do you have a gift registry?

Your presence is the greatest gift! If you'd like to give something, a monetary gift is very much appreciated. Details on where to send it are in the Gifts section on the Home tab.

 Who do I contact if I have more questions?

Reach out to Kamille or Vlarix directly — we're happy to help with anything. You can also message our wedding coordinator for venue and logistics questions.
