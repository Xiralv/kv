FAQ

Guests & RSVP

Can I bring a plus one?

Your invitation is made out to the specific guests listed on your RSVP. If a plus one is included, they'll appear in the RSVP flow when you enter your name. If they're not listed, we kindly ask that only the named guests attend — we have a set number of seats.

 Are children welcome?

We love your little ones! Children are welcome at the ceremony. For the reception, we'd love for it to be an adults-only evening so everyone can celebrate freely — we hope you understand and can arrange childcare for the night.

 When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by November 30, 2026 using the button at the bottom of this page. If you have any trouble, message Kamille or Vlarix directly and we'll sort it out!

On the day

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony begins at 3:00 PM sharp — please don't be late! We recommend arriving at least 15–20 minutes early to find your seat. Doors open from 2:30 PM.

 Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We're having an unplugged ceremony — please keep phones and cameras away so everyone can be fully present. Our photographer will capture everything! Feel free to take as many photos as you like at the reception. 📸

 What should I wear?

Principal Sponsors — Ladies in Champagne or Nude long dress. Gentlemen in Barong (long or short sleeve), any pants except khaki.

Guests — Ladies in Sage Green, Dusty Blue, or Light Brown long dress or pantsuit. Gentlemen in Barong (long or short sleeve), any pants except khaki.

Please avoid casual attire, loud prints, and reserve white and khaki for the couple. 🤍

Getting there

Is parking available?

Our Mother of Perpetual Help Parish has limited street parking. We recommend using Grab or a taxi to the church so you don't have to worry about it. Waterfront Insular Hotel has its own parking for the reception.

 Is there a shuttle between church and reception?

Yes! We're arranging a shuttle from Our Mother of Perpetual Help Parish to Waterfront Insular Hotel Davao after the ceremony. Timing details will be shared closer to the date.

Gifts & contact

Do you have a gift registry?

Your presence is the greatest gift! If you'd like to give something, a monetary gift is very much appreciated. Details on where to send it are in the Gifts section of this app.

 Who do I contact if I have more questions?

Reach out to Kamille or Vlarix directly — we're happy to help with anything. You can also message our wedding coordinator for venue and logistics questions.
